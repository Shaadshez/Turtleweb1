Bentota Hatchery

Introducing Bentota Hatchery: A Sanctuary for Sea Turtles in Sri Lanka

Welcome to Bentota Hatchery, a haven nestled along Sri Lanka's picturesque coastline. This sanctuary is dedicated to the preservation and conservation of sea turtles. Here, visitors can witness the remarkable journey of these ancient mariners as they nest, hatch, and make their way back to the ocean. Bentota Hatchery is not only a place of conservation but also education. Through guided tours and interactive programs, visitors can learn about the challenges faced by sea turtles and become advocates for their protection. Join us at Bentota Hatchery, where you can experience the magic of new life, the beauty of nature, and make a positive impact in safeguarding the future of these magnificent creatures.

Hatchery Tours

Kosgoda Hatchery is open to visitors and offers guided tours of their facilities. Visitors can learn about the different species of sea turtles and see the hatchlings up close. Visitors to Kosgoda Hatchery can take guided tours of the facilities to learn about the different species of sea turtles found in Sri Lanka and the conservation efforts being undertaken to protect them. The hatchlings are kept in tanks until they are strong enough to be released into the ocean. Visitors can see the hatchlings up close and even participate in the release of baby turtles into the sea. The hatchery also conducts research on sea turtles and their habitats, as well as outreach programs to local communities to promote sea turtle conservation. By visiting Kosgoda Hatchery, visitors can learn about the important work being done to protect these beautiful creatures and contribute to their conservation efforts.
