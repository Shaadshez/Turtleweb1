Welcome to Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

Located on the southern coast of Sri Lanka, Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a sanctuary for sea turtles.

Gallery

Hikkaduwa Hatchery

The Hikkaduwa Hatchery is a popular destination for those who want to learn more about the conservation efforts around turtles. The hatchery rescues and rehabilitates different species of turtles such as Olive Ridley, Green, Hawksbill, and Loggerhead turtles. Here are some things you can do at the hatchery:

What We Can Do with Turtles

  • Learn about the different species of turtles that are rescued and rehabilitated at the hatchery
  • Observe the turtles in their tanks and learn about their behavior and life cycle
  • Participate in the release of the turtles back into the ocean, usually at night when it is safer for them
  • Support the conservation efforts of the hatchery by making a donation or purchasing souvenirs
